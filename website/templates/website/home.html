About STAS

Nurturing Innovation Since Inception

STAS Kottayam is a constituent unit of the Centre for Professional and Advanced Studies (CPAS), established by the Government of Kerala. We focus on delivering high-quality education in technical and applied science fields, ensuring our students are industry-ready from day one.

  • State-of-the-art Computer Labs with high-speed connectivity.
  • Expert Faculty members with extensive industry experience.
  • Robust Placement Cell with 90%+ recruitment rate.
